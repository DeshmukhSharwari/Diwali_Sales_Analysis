**Customer Purchase Analysis Dataset**

 * Project Overview: 
This project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior based on demographic details,
 product categories, and order values. The dataset helps in understanding customer segments, sales trends,
and buying patterns across different regions and occupations.

* Dataset Description: 
The dataset contains customer-level transactional data with demographic and product information.

 * Columns Explanation: 
User_ID – Unique identifier for each customer
Cust_name – Name of the customer
Product_ID – Unique identifier for the product
Gender – Gender of the customer
Age Group – Age category of the customer (e.g., 18–25, 26–35)
Age – Actual age of the customer
Marital_Status – Marital status (Single / Married)
State – State where the customer resides
Zone – Geographical zone (North, South, East, West, Central)
Occupation – Profession or occupation of the customer
Product_Category – Category of the purchased product
Orders – Number of orders placed by the customer
Amount – Total purchase amount spent by the customer

* Objectives: 
Analyze customer demographics and purchasing patterns
Identify high-value customer segments
Understand sales distribution across states, zones, and occupations
Evaluate product category performance

* Tools & Technologies: 
Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
Power BI (for dashboard and visualization)
Jupyter Notebook

* Insights & Analysis: 
Customer distribution by age group and gender
Order and revenue analysis by product category
Regional sales performance (State & Zone wise)
Occupation-based purchasing behavior


* Conclusion: 
This analysis provides meaningful insights into customer behavior and sales trends, which can help businesses improve targeting, inventory planning, and marketing strategies.
